What is Product Management?

Product Management is the art of building the right product for the right people. It combines business, technology, and user experience to deliver real value.

💡 What does a Product Manager do?

Vision

Defines what the product should become and why it matters.

Strategy

Decides what to build, when, and how it creates value.

Execution

Works with engineers and designers to ship features.

🚀 Key Skills

Communication

Aligns teams and stakeholders effectively.

Problem Solving

Understands user pain points deeply.

Data Thinking

Uses metrics to guide decisions.

⚙️ Product Process

Discover

Research users and identify problems.

Define

Prioritize and plan solutions.

Deliver

Build, launch, and iterate quickly.
